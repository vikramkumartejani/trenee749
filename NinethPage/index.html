Brian White

Co--Founder andChief Brand Officer

The prolific Emmy-nominated Brian White is a star of stage and screen. He has starred in critically acclaimed films such as Stomp the Yard, The Family Stone, The Game Plan, 12 Rounds, I Can Do Bad All by Myself, Good Deeds, The Cabin in the Woods, Men of a Certain Age, Beauty and the Beast, Scandal, and Ambitions on television. He has multiple nominations for a Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Digital Daytime Drama Series for Bronx SIU in 2019 and 2020. Aside from his vast and illustrious acting career, White joined forces with several business partners to create the ultimate app for entrepreneurs and those who "hustle," Preneur.ai. The app debuted in early 2021. The Preneur app is working to democratize entrepreneurship by bringing Fortune 500-level software, distribution, and community to individuals and small businesses.
